BANGLADESH INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL & COLLEGE

INTRODUCTION

The name of my school is BISC. It is located in Nirjhor Dhaka,Cantonment. My school is a big 3 storey building with 3 big playgrounds. There are basketball court, football field, cricket field, volleyball court etc. There are also a big library, labs and indoor games. The teachers are very friendly and highly qualified. There are total of 5000 students in my school.

FACILITIES

There are so much facilities in our school.

  • Library:A well furnished and specious library is being installed. In near future, the book worms will have a large collection of books of various interests and the facilities of a digital library.
  • Canteen:There is an arrangement for students and guardians canteen for provision of healthy and delicious food, snacks, and soft drink. Stationary items are also available here.
  • Guardian Shed:There is an earmarked facility for the parents waiting for their kids to drop and pick up. They one can easily communicate with school staff. Besides interacting with class teachers, parents can also socialize with other parents as well.
  • Facilities of Games And Sports:Periodically, school authority also facilitates various enrichment program like teachers and child grooming, sharing experience of academic interest etc. The institution possesses a very big playground. There are regular games classes for the students. There are scopes for playing football, cricket, handball, badminton etc. in the spacious field.
  • Prayer Room:Designated prayer room for teachers and students is another hallmark of our school. The facilities provide convenient opportunities to offer prayer within the campus and enhance ethical values among the students.

HISTORY

The school was established on 14 March 1995 under the dynamic leadership of the founder Principal in Charge Mr. A K M Sufiur Rahman with only 27 students. The first H.S.C national curriculum batch of students started in 2011 with 15 students. The batch was inaugurated on 17 September 2011 and the school was renamed Bangladesh International School and College.
